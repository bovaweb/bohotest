Our Story

A Rooftop Born
from Wanderlust

Tucked above the lively streets of Garia, right beside Kavi Nazrul Metro Station, Boho The Sky Café was born from a simple dream — to create a space where South Kolkata's youth could breathe, feast, and feel free.

We blend bohemian soul with rooftop luxury. Hanging greens, warm fairy lights, rustic wood, and a horizon that turns gold at dusk — this is where birthdays are celebrated, dates become evenings to remember, and friendships are deepened over great food.

From crispy thin-crust pizzas to creamy mocktails, our menu is crafted to delight — casual enough for everyday hangouts, elevated enough for every occasion.
